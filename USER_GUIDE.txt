Legendary Photobooth User Guide

To set up:
    * plug in monitor hdmi to raspberry pi 
    * plug in monitor's usb power (the monitor does have a battery, but it may not last long)
    * plug in big red button to raspberry pi (usb)
    * plug in power to raspberry pi (usb c)
        * After plugging in the power the computer will boot up and automatically open the photo booth app

To take selfie:
    * after following setup instructions and the camera preview is showing on screen, press the big red button

To shutdown or restart:
    * plug in mouse
    * click to close photo booth window
    * click the raspberry button in the top left
        * click logout
            * click shutdown or restart

View photos:
    * photos are stored in folders by date on the desktop
